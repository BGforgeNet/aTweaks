Death Knight's Aura of Fear

A Death Knight constantly generates fear. Any susceptible creature within range must make a saving throw versus spell or flee in panic for 4 rounds.

Ghast's Carrion Stench

Ghasts exude a carrion stench in a 10' feet radius which causes retching and nausea unless a saving throw vs poison is made. Those failing their save will attack at a penalty of -2.

Ghoul Lord's Evil Aura

Ghoul Lords reek fairly of evil. Those of good alignment suffer a -4 penalty on attack rolls when within 30' of this creature. A remove fear spell negates the effects of this foul aura.

Mummy's Aura of Fear

The mere sight of a mummy causes such terror in any creature that a saving throw versus spell must be made or the victim becomes incapacitated with fright for 2 rounds. Humans save against mummies with a +2 bonus. The enormous power and presence of the Greater Mummy forces all creatures who see them to make a save versus spell with a -3 penalty or become frozen with fear for 4 rounds.

Skeleton Warrior's Aura of Fear

The mere sight of a Skeleton Warrior causes any creature with fewer than 5 HD to flee in panic for 5 rounds.

Symbol, Pain

A symbol spell creates magical runes affecting creatures that pass over, touch, or read the runes, or pass through a portal upon which the symbol is inscribed. Symbol, Pain afflicts all creatures in the area of effect with wracking pains shooting through their bodies, causing a 2 point penalty to Dexterity and a 4 point penalty to attack rolls during the following 10 turns. Multiple castings of this spell are not cumulative.

Wall of Ice

This spell can be cast in one of three ways: as an anchored plane of ice, as a hemisphere, or as a horizontal sheet to fall upon creatures with the effect of an ice storm.
   Ice sheet. This casting of the spell causes a horizontal sheet to fall upon opponents. The sheet covers a 10-foot-square area per caster level. The sheet has the same effects as an ice storm's hail stones--3d10 points of damage inflicted to creatures beneath it.
